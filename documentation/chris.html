User Story:

chris loves to listen to music and heard about an app that connects you to people that love the same music as you.

Persona:

  • Name:chris
  • Age:25
  • Family Status:Single but has kids
  • Profession:Is a Semi-truck driver.
  • Tech Use:Iphone 11 /macbook pro
  • Attitudes and Behaviors: chris loves music he listens to a lot of different music and podcast well on the road .His pretty much on the road 24/7 so all he has is his music and is in a different town Overnight.
  • Frustrations: Chris wants to find a girl that has the same taste as him .And since he is in a different town Overnight he wants to find people near him that likes the same music/podcasts
  • Goals:chris would just love to find like-minded people in the town he is currently at.

inter-action flow for messaging

  • A little message window pops up where you can write anything you want .User writes a little message introducing himself and then clicks send.
  • Other profile receives a message saying they got a pending message and shows users profile and a button if you want to accept and reply or reject and or block .
  • Other user decides to accept it and reads the message and reply's and then hits the send button to send message
  • User receives alert saying they got 1 new message/reply.User clicks reply button and then replays to message and hits the button send .
  • Both users meet another in real life and live happily ever after .
