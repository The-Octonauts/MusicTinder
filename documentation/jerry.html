User Story:

Jerry is a lonely, lonely man who happens to really like music. He needs to find a date to the winter social asap who hopefully likes the Misfits as much as he does.

Persona:

  • Name:Jerry Barry
  • Age:33
  • Family Status:Single/Never married but has 2 kids
  • Profession:Manager of guitar center
  • Tech Use:Iphone 11
  • Attitudes and Behaviors: Jerry loves music. He loves to be around it and loves to listen to it. He is the manager of a busy music store in a major metropolitan area. He likes a very unique subset of music called Djent and he has trouble finding others in his area that like it as well. Jerry has 2 kids from 2 different baby mamas so his love life is a mess, possibly like him.
  • Frustrations: Jerry wants to find "the one" who can listen to Djent and is "not like the other girls" but he just cannot find anyone in his local friends group that likes this kind of horrible music. He is fed up that not even other men can appreciate this genre and hopes to find the girl of his dreams using online dating apps.
  • Goals:Jerry needs to find a girl that likes Djent or Djent adjacent music.

Home/Recommendations page flow

  • Once the initial login is complete, user is sent directly to recommendations page every time they log in.
  • Music tinder shows profiles of people they match with .
  • User swipes the screen right if they like the person they are recommended and swipes left if they do not like the recommendation.
  • Music tinder keeps showing recommendations until there are no more recommendations OR the user stops swiping.
  • User then waits to get matched with another users and leads them to the messaging flow.

Music/podcast selection flow

  • User enters page with a selection of the top 100 podcasts on Spotify
  • Site prompts user to select 5 of their favorite podcasts with a ranking 1-5
  • User selects their 5 top podcasts
  • Site redirects them to a recommendations page.

Interaction Flow:

  1. Client Downloads App
  2. App shows a link to connect Spotify account
  3. Jerry clicks link and enters his information for Spotify within the Music tinder app
  4. App returns a screen showing successful connection to Spotify
  5. User clicks "next" button to move to the profile screen
  6. App leads user to profile screen and prompts them to add a picture and contact info
  7. User adds a picture from his desktop and his mobile number to his connected profile
  8. App accepts or rejects user inputs and leads them to main screen that shows them pictures of other people who like the same music.
  9. User swipes right to "like" or swipes "left" to reject a person based on their picture.
  10. App matches user with those that they swiped right on and matches them.
  11. User gets a message saying they matched with person on the app and is prompted to message the other user within the app
  12. App sends the other user a message showing that Jerry wants to meet
  13. Both users agree to meet at their local concert
