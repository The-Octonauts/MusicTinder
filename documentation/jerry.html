User Story:

Jerry is a lonely, lonely man who happens to really like music. He needs to find a date to the winter social asap who hopefully likes the Misfits as much as he does.

Persona:

  • Name:Jerry Barry
  • Age:33
  • Family Status:Single/Never married but has kids
  • Profession:Manager of guitar center
  • Tech Use:Iphone 11
  • Attitudes and Behaviors: Jerry loves music. He loves to be around it and loves to listen to it. He likes a very unique subset of music called Djent and he has trouble finding others in his area that like it as well. Jerry has 2 kids from 2 different baby mamas so his love life is a mess
  • Frustrations: Jerry wants to find "the one" who can listen to Djent and is "not like the other girls" but he just cannot find anyone in his local friends group that likes this kind of horrible music. He is fed up that not even other men can appreciate this genre and hopes to find the girl of his dreams using online dating apps.
  • Goals:Jerry needs to find a girl that likes Djent or Djent adjacent music.
